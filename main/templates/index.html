The Titanic Disaster

On April 15, 1912, the RMS Titanic sank after striking an iceberg during her maiden voyage. Of the 2,224 passengers and crew aboard, more than 1,500 perished, making it one of the deadliest peacetime maritime disasters in history.

Our machine learning model analyzes historical passenger data to understand the factors that influenced survival rates, including passenger class, gender, age, and family relationships.
